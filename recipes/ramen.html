Kewpie Mayo Instant Ramen Hack

The ramen hack trending on tiktok!

This easy ramen hack only requires a few ingredients but tastes amazing!

Ingredients

  • One pack instant ramen, spicy recommended (I used the brand Jin Ramen)
  • 1 pack dried instant ramen seasoning
  • 1 tbsp kewpie mayo
  • 500 ml hot water
  • 1 raw egg
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced or grated
  • Optional: 1 tbsp scallions, finely sliced

Steps

  1. In a serving bowl, add minced/grated garlic, kewpie mayo, raw egg and the dried instant ramen seasoning packet. Mix well.
  2. Then in a pot with 500 ml of water (or the amount specified on the instant ramen packet if the packet calls for less), bring to a boil. Add instant ramen noodles and boil for 2 minutes.
  3. Pour half of the hot ramen water into your serving bowl with the mayo egg mixture and mix well.
  4. Then pour the rest of the ramen water and ramen noodles. Mix until the ramen and broth together and enjoy! Garnish with scallions, optional.
